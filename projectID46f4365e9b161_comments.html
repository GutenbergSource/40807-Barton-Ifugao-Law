This interesting study concentrates on the customary laws of the Ifugao, a Philippine people living in the high-lands of Northern Luzon, refuting the idea (common in 1919) that 'tribal' people are wild and law-less.

The copy of this book (actually an off-print from a magazine article) has some heavy underlining, which should be ignored, but sometimes hurts the OCR.
